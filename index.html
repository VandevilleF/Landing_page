MYLIB

Manage your library, anytime, anywhere !

Features

Display and sort books

Dynamically displays a list of the user's books, with cover, title and author.

Organize the book list according to choice, whether by title, author or date added.

By clicking on the "details" button, you'll be redirected to the book page containing more information.

Book information

Displays more detailed information about the book (title, author, publisher and release date).

Gives an overview of the book's reading status (read, unread, in progress).

Book notes can be taken and displayed directly in the book information.

Pressing the "Supprimer" button removes the book from the user's list.

A summary of the book can be consulted in the "Résumé" tab of the book information.

Search and add books to the list

Find books based on their title or author name.

By entering the ISBN number or scanning it, you can perform a search.

Simply tick the box to add to the library.

Most of the information in the book is provided.

User account management and statistics.

Users can easily change their name, e-mail address or password by selecting the appropriate tab.

Account deletion in just a few clicks.

Real-time statistical analysis of readings.

About

It all started with a simple observation: my girlfriend had trouble remembering the books she owned. She often found herself taking photos of her bookshelf or using post-it notes to write down the titles of the books she had. These solutions were impractical and often ineffective. That's how the idea for MyLib was born.

MyLib is an application designed to help you easily manage your personal library. With MyLib, you can add or remove books from your collection in a snap, and quickly display detailed information about the books you own. Say goodbye to forgetfulness and unnecessary duplicates—your library is always at your fingertips.
